What do you need to part with?

"Now where did I leave that...?" is a common thought. But do you ever remember where you stored something, but not the thing you stored?

I find that writing has this effect sometimes. There are many "shoulds" that await me in my planner, but I cannot recall them individually.

This is a space to write down what no longer serves you in order to unfasten it from your memory.

You may leave behind a memory, a habit, a piece of information you wish you never heard, or anything of the sort.

Once you submit the form below, your forgotten will be stored in a text file, which in turn will be stored in a USB that I frequently wear on a beaded string around my neck.

I'll carry this forgotten for you until it hopefully becomes just that.
